About Run Wild

Run Wild Trail Camp aims to empower the campers and their families to get excited about the environment, staying active, and investing in lasting change. Through working with the local community and resources, we hope to provide the campers with sustainable resources and valuable experiences they can hold with them and apply to their daily lives once camp is over.
